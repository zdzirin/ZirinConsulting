Consulting and Actuarial Services

Working With You

Zirin Consulting, LLC provides independent actuarial consulting services for insurance company and self-insured clients. Current projects include work on year-end statements of actuarial opinion for large and small insurers, peer review of self-insured program actuarial analyses, and captive feasibility studies.

With over 30 years of actuarial experience in both consulting and insurance company management, I can effectively communicate technical results to senior management and key decision-makers. As a strategic thinker I can help your organization in developing and implementing your business plans. Experience includes work on most commercial and personal lines of business, with a focus on workers compensation, medical professional liability, general liability, commercial auto and surety.
