Zirin Consulting, LLC provides independent actuarial consulting services for insurance company and self-insured clients. Current projects include work on year-end statements of actuarial opinion for large and small insurers, peer review of self-insured program actuarial analyses, and captive feasibility studies.

With over 30 years of actuarial experience in both consulting and insurance company management, I can effectively communicate technical results to senior management and key decision-makers. As a strategic thinker I can help your organization in developing and implementing your business plans. Experience includes:

  • Reserve review and evaluation for all Property & Casualty lines of business

  • Statutory statement of actuarial opinion preparation

  • Board and management reporting

  • Excess insurance evaluation

  • Captive evaluation and feasibility studies

  • Cost allocation studies

  • Peer Review services

  • Financial Planning and long term models

  • Risk and capital models
